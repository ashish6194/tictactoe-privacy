Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

Introduction

Tic Tac Toe Online ("we", "our", "the app") is a free mobile game. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

Anonymous Game Data: We generate a random anonymous player ID stored locally on your device for online multiplayer functionality. This ID is not linked to your identity.

Game Scores: Win/loss/draw statistics are stored locally on your device only.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob - for displaying advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase - for online multiplayer game rooms. Only anonymous player IDs and game board states are stored. See Supabase Privacy Policy.

Data Storage

Game scores and preferences are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. Online game room data is temporarily stored on Supabase servers and automatically deleted after 1 hour.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require account creation or any personal information.

Advertising

We display ads through Google AdMob. You may see personalized or non-personalized ads based on your device settings. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings.

No Account Required

Our app does not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or share any personal information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated date at the top of this page.
